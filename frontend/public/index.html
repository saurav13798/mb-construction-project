Founder & CEO

Mahesh Bhosale

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 20 years of experience in the construction industry, Mahesh founded MB Construction with a vision to revolutionize infrastructure development in Maharashtra. His expertise in project management and government relations has been instrumental in securing major contracts and delivering exceptional results.

Under his leadership, MB Construction has grown from a small local contractor to a trusted partner for large-scale redevelopment projects and government infrastructure initiatives. Mahesh holds a degree in Civil Engineering and is known for his commitment to quality, safety, and innovation.
