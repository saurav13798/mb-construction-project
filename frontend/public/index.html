Meet Our Leadership

Visionary leadership driving excellence in construction

Founder & CEO

Mahesh Bhosale

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

A visionary leader with over 15 years of experience in infrastructure development, Mahesh founded MB Construction in 2009 with a mission to revolutionize engineering quality and project delivery standards. His strategic expertise in complex project management, government relations, and sustainable construction practices has established MB Construction as Maharashtra's premier contractor for mission-critical infrastructure projects.

Starting from humble beginnings with a small team of 5 engineers, Mahesh has grown MB Construction into a technology-driven organization employing over 500 professionals across multiple states. His commitment to innovation led to the early adoption of BIM (Building Information Modeling), IoT-enabled construction monitoring, and green building technologies, setting industry benchmarks for efficiency and sustainability.

Mahesh holds a Master's degree in Civil Engineering from IIT Mumbai and advanced certifications in Project Management (PMP), Sustainable Construction (LEED AP), and Government Contracting. His leadership philosophy centers on empowering teams, embracing technological advancement, and maintaining unwavering commitment to safety and quality. Under his guidance, MB Construction has achieved a remarkable 98% client satisfaction rate and zero major safety incidents across all projects.

Beyond business success, Mahesh is passionate about skill development in the construction industry. He regularly conducts workshops for young engineers and has established the MB Construction Skills Academy, providing free training to underprivileged youth in construction trades. His vision extends to making construction more sustainable, efficient, and inclusive for future generations.
